Process

How it works

Every pitch that reaches an allocator has already survived the sender's selection process — so its backtest is, by construction, an upper bound. The only defensible response is adversarial validation.

01

Send the trade series

Per-trade CSV (timestamps, side, size, fills, fees) or a daily returns series (reduced scope). Your logic and code never leave the building. Optional NDA at scoping.

02

The harness runs

Deflated Sharpe at your declared trial count and at adversarial counts, five-tier cost stress, chronological derivation/validation split, regime folds, selection-bias screens.

03

Verdict in 5 business days

A fixed-scope report — REAL / FRAGILE / THIN / OVERFIT — with the evidence math shown, plus a 30-min walkthrough call.

Deliverables

What the report contains

  1. Deflated Sharpe Ratioat your declared trial count and at stress-tested counts — the single strongest test against multiple-testing bias.
  2. Five-tier cost stress10+2 → 30+10 bps per leg: the exact fee/slippage level at which the edge dies.
  3. Walk-forward & regime stabilitydoes the edge live in one period or all of them?
  4. Selection-bias screensin-sample threshold picks, calendar-window restrictions, thin-tail concentration, derivation-vs-holdout deltas.
  5. Classification + upgrade paththe verdict, and the minimum additional evidence that would upgrade it.
Classification

The verdict scale

Mechanical rules, published in the report. We sell the verdict, not flattery — the sample below is an audit of our own strategy, and it did not pass.

REAL

Survives Deflated Sharpe at adversarial trial counts, deep cost tiers, an honest holdout and every screen — on an evidence base of ≥100 trades.

FRAGILE

Certifies at the declared trial count, but dies under adversarial trial assumptions or deeper cost tiers.

THIN

Positive — possibly genuinely so — but the evidence base cannot support certification. "Probably real but unprovable" is THIN, not REAL.

OVERFIT

The record contradicts itself: the holdout slice fails, or critical selection screens fire. The backtest measured the search, not the market.

FAQ

Questions

Do you see my strategy logic?

No. The submission is a trade series only: timestamps, symbol, side, size, fill prices, fees. That is sufficient for every test we run and insufficient to reverse-engineer a signal.

What data format do you accept?

CSV. Full scope: per-trade records (entry_ts, exit_ts, symbol, side, entry_price, exit_price, optional qty and fee_pct). Reduced scope: a daily returns series (date, ret) — note that cost stress and trade-level screens require per-trade records, so returns-only audits are capped below REAL.

Who is this for?

Allocators and fund-of-funds screening external managers; emerging funds that want an independent validation exhibit for their DD pack; trading teams that want a pre-deployment gate for their own research.

Why should I trust the harness?

Because we pointed it at ourselves first. It evaluated 5,947 of our own models and certified none; it killed 191 of ~192 pre-registered hypotheses — including the ones we most wanted to believe. The one strategy it certified has been positive in every calendar year of a five-year window. The full story, numbers included, is in the whitepaper.

Is my data confidential?

Yes — used solely for the audit, never shared, deleted on request after delivery. An NDA is available at the scoping stage.
